The Problem

Patient portals do a really bad job of helping people to see how their health data affects their day-to-day life. Consequently, people trying to improve their health don’t have the tools and knowledge they need to make safe, long lasting changes. And with a limited amount of willpower it’s tough to just “keep going”. We believe these are big problems so we're doing something about it. 

What We Do

Kurbi is a web and mobile app that helps people living with health related limitations to do things they care about, safely.

How it works is simple :: based on what we know about our user we show them how their health will affect their ability to accomplish their goals.
